Vivyan Woods

She's an aspiring author currently going to the University of Washington to study Informatics. As a kid, she had always loved to create stories and worlds for them. It wasn't until high school when she decided to start writing them down. From there, she wrote every day, writing over 50 short stories. Summer of her 16th year, she decided that she wanted to expand on one of her short stories and wrote her first novel. Although she's busy studying at her university, she's still in progress of writing a second novel.
